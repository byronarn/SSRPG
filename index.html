SSRPG

The Super Simple Role Playing Game

Introduction

Most Role Playing Games (RPGs) are just too complex. They have way too many stats and rules for newbies to remember, and as a result it's easy for players to get bogged down with rules and forget what RPGs are supposed to be about: Having fun. Rule books can often be hundreds of pages per book, with several books to explain and flesh out the rules. SSRPG was developed to counter this.

The Super Simple Role Playing Game (SSRPG) is a very easy to learn and play Role Playing Game. It is ideal for children and newbies to tabletop gaming. All you need to play is one or more six sided dice, a Game Master (GM), one or more Player Characters (PCs), and paper and pencil. The "rule book" is a PDF that is only 5 pages long! There is only one stat to keep track of: SKILL.

I developed this RPG in order to introduce my children to RPGs. We've had countless hours of fun playing it, and I've fine tuned the rules during this gameplay. I would like to present it to the world in case their are others like me who want to teach their children the art of playing RPGs without having to get bogged down by hundreds of pages of rules. Feel free to download SSRPG and play it. Send me any questions and/or comments, to help me improve the game!
